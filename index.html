About me

Hi, I'm Miroslav Mihok, a 29-year-old programmer. Originally from Slovakia but moved to Austria when I was 11. It was during my time at HAK in Neusiedl am See that I began my journey into programming, focusing on languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Today, I'm a dedicated programmer who has reignited my passion for coding. I've explored technologies such as React, Angular, and TypeScript, continuously creating and learning. I enjoy solving problems and find great satisfaction in the moment when a complex puzzle is finally pieced together.

I believe in following your passion, and for me, that passion is coding. Here's to pursuing dreams, writing code, and shaping the digital world, one project at a time.

Boosting Service (February - April 2024)

Boosting Service is a platform for gamers seeking personalized boosting services, this project showcases my ability to create a user-friendly interface that allows visitors to easily navigate and customize their experience. Users can select their desired game, filter products, add items to their cart, and securely complete transactions via Stripe or PayPal.

Boosting Service offers gamers to register as Booster or Coach through a "work with us" form.

Behind the scenes, a fully functional authentication system and Admin Panel provide seamless management of Products, Games, Users, and Orders. Each user has their profile, allowing them to manage personal information, view order history, and complete pending tasks.
